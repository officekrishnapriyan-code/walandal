Systems We Build

wal and al develops applied systems that combine robotics, ROS / ROS 2, and cloud platforms. Our work translates environmental and operational signals into reliable, decision-ready intelligence for teams on the ground.

Aqua Insight ‚Äì Drinking Water Intelligence

A monitoring and insight layer for drinking water networks, focused on overhead tanks and local distribution in rural and urban settings. Aqua Insight provides continuous visibility into water quality and availability.

  • Tracking of key parameters such as chlorine, pH, turbidity, TDS, and level.
  • Alerting when conditions drift toward risk or non-compliance zones.
  • Dashboards and reports that support operations, maintenance, and planning.

Industrial Emission Monitoring Layer

A data and analytics layer for understanding emissions around industrial sites and corridors. The system focuses on measuring pollutants and trends that matter for compliance, risk, and long-term environmental stewardship.

  • Measurement of critical gases, particulates, and carbon-related indicators.
  • Structured insights for boundary, corridor, and zone-based assessment.
  • Cloud-native reporting and APIs for regulators, operators, and auditors.

Marine Environmental Observation Systems

Systems designed to capture environmental signals in coastal and near-shore waters. These solutions help teams understand changing marine conditions and support monitoring, planning, and research.

  • Acquisition of key physical and chemical indicators in marine environments.
  • Detection of shifts linked to pollution, stress, or resilience.
  • Data products ready for analysis, mapping, and scientific workflows.

ROS / ROS 2 Fleet & Cloud Integration

An integration layer that connects ROS and ROS 2-based systems with modern cloud infrastructure. The goal is to make fleets observable, manageable, and easier to evolve over time.

  • Telemetry, logging, and health monitoring for distributed robotic systems.
  • Bridges between ROS topics and cloud services for storage, analytics, and control.
  • Foundations for remote operations, updates, and digital-twin style analysis.
