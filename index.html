The WAL & AL framework.

02 / WHAT WE BUILD

Systems engineered for the field.

CLOUD-NATIVE

Aqua Insight

Drinking Water Intelligence

A monitoring and insight layer for drinking water networks. Continuous, real-time visibility into water quality and availability — from overhead tanks to the last mile of local distribution.

  • Real-time chlorine, pH, turbidity, TDS, level
  • Automated alerting for risk and non-compliance zones
  • Dashboards for operations, maintenance, planning

EDGE INTELLIGENCE

Industrial Emission

Monitoring Layer

A data and analytics layer for emissions monitoring around industrial sites and corridors. Measuring the pollutants and trends that matter most for compliance, stewardship, and community health.

  • Gases, particulates, carbon indicators
  • Boundary and zone assessment
  • Cloud-native reporting and APIs for regulators

AUTONOMOUS

Marine Observation

Environmental Systems

Autonomous systems capturing environmental signals in coastal and near-shore waters. Built for conditions where traditional infrastructure can't go — supporting monitoring, planning, and research at scale.

  • Key physical and chemical marine indicators
  • Detection of pollution and stress shifts
  • Data products for analysis and mapping

ROS / ROS 2

Fleet & Cloud

Integration Layer

An integration layer connecting ROS and ROS 2-based robotic systems with modern cloud infrastructure. Observable fleets, reliable telemetry, and remote operations — all in one place.

  • Telemetry, logging, health monitoring
  • Bridges between ROS topics and cloud services
  • Remote operations and digital-twin analysis
